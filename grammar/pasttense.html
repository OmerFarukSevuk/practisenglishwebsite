Used to And Would

Affirmative:
I used to/would play a lot when I was small.

Negative:
She didn’t use tohave so many exams.

Question:
What did you use to do?

We use used to and would to talk about past habits, things we did regularly in the past, but not now. We cannot use would for past states or situations, only for past actions. To talk about past habits, we don’t usually use would in the negative or question form.

I used to/would ride my bike to school when I was small.
I would play with my toys.
